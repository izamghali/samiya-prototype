CV. Cakra Anagata Gemilang is a company engaged in Export-Import and General Trading, located in Majalengka, West Java with a huge network of any commodity suppliers in Indonesia and worldwide. Our company has complete license and legality as a Export-Import and General Trading company. Our company also fully supported and cooperates with other companies to help farmers, breeders, SMEs, to be able to market their products nationally and worldwide CV. Cakra Anagata Gemilang very selective when choosing our suppliers and dealers. We deeply hold on to our company core values, which are: Integrity, Quality, Commitment, and Trust.
